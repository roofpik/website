Wonderful ambience - greenery all around

One entry and exit point has been constructed so thats a very big advantage and guards are always present. Clubhouse is there and it has a good ambience and is very well maintained. There are wide open green areas. It is spread across the Sohna Road and Golf Course Road Extension. There are some problems with the power backup. Housemaids are easily available. Basement and open parking is maintained and twice a day they have guards around. Here we have high-rise buildings with very good layout, main road connectivity is good which provides for easy transportation. Sohna Road is a problem as service roads are bad if you are coming in or going out of the society it is bad but inside, the ambience is wonderful.
