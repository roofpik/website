What is Roofpik?

Roofpik is the first-of-its-kind real estate community that has thousands of property reviews by verified residents. On Roofpik, residents and home buyers can directly share their personal experiences of buying, renting, investing, and living in a property. This helps Roofpik users find their dream home by comparing and selecting from hundreds of properties.

How does Roofpik help my search for a home?

Roofpik helps you directly connect with real residents who are sharing first-hand experiences about a property or real estate project. Roofpik props up this useful information by showing you comparative listings, refined search results, verified dealers, and more. This helps you make well-informed decisions on finding a great property in the locality of your choice.

How is Roofpik different from other property search portals?

Unlike other property search portals that lay emphasis on property listings and classifieds, Roofpik is focused on genuine reviews, validated project information, and trusted dealers/partners. Roofpik offers:

  • Reviews: Real residents and property owners sharing positive and negative experiences
  • Project information: Everything that you need to choose a property including photos, comparisons, and price trends.
  • Resident stories: Read real experiences of people about their property and their detailed insights about the project.
  • Facilities in the vicinity: Everything you and your family need to roll, from work to play and from utilities to transport.
  • Listed partners: A direct channel to connect with verified dealers such as brokers, packers and movers, furniture rentals, and more.

What kind of properties can I search for on Roofpik?

You can find user reviews for both apartments and independent homes as well as for localities.

Who can see what I share on Roofpik?

Roofpik’s rapidly growing community of residents and property buyers, sellers, and owners can see what you publicly share on Roofpik.
