What does it take to be a Roofian?

At Roofpik, we hire the smartest, most passionate people who don’t shy away from challenges. We love to encourage our employees to use their time to not just develop their own skills and talents, but also to grow as individuals on their journey with us. If you’re ambitious, have an entrepreneurial streak, and appreciate the difference between smart work and hard work, we want to hear from you.



The Building Blocks of Life at Roofpik

Development

We are focused on creating a work environment that thrives on discussions, reasoning, and actions that help solve real problems. Roofians are driven by a high sense of independence to confidently spearhead unconventional approaches to everyday challenges.

Creativity

We believe that empowered people can redefine the way a problem is solved. That’s precisely why creativity through empowerment is an inseparable part of life at Roofpik. At Roofpik, creativity also means striving to be counted among the best and the brightest.

Culture

At Roofpik, professionalism, empowerment, honesty, accountability, and team work are more than just buzzwords – they are our core values and a way of life. The Roofpik culture is all about sharing the same enthusiasm for our colleagues’ ambitions as we do for our own.
